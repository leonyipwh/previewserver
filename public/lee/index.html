We have now entered the “Me” economy. People are no longer content with just being a voice; they want to be a brand. Lee decided to help people show off their unique brands by letting them take over the most iconic feature on any pair of jeans… The leather patch.






Introducing “My Laser-made Patch”

Through our Internet-connected laser-engraving system, we made making a leather patch as simple as taking a photo. We've developed a technology solution that utilises hardware-software integration, control automation, an image processing system and WeChat integration. This has dramatically reduced the complexity of the leather patch-making process from a highly-manual, heavy-lifting process to a single click.



The making of “My Leather Patch”

1. User uploads photos remotely.
2. Our Internet-connected laser-engraving machine activated in store.
3. The uniquely branded jeans are ready for pick-up.



Result



It became so popular that Lee decided to turn this seasonal campaign into a permanent retail service for the whole of China.
