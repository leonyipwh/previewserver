CHALLENGE: Everyone looks the same


In today’s fast-fashion world, how could we make AEO’s latest ripped
jeans collection stand out from the competition while allowing youths to stay unique? 

SOLUTION: A new way to wear ripped jeans: the AEO Rip Tattoo.





1. First, we sent the AEO Live Express Truck to major shopping areas, equipped with a State-of-the-Art Voice Recognition System.

2. Then, we asked people what words they live by and recorded their life mottos.

3. Recordings were immediately turned into original tattoo designs that reflect and celebrate their unique personalities.

4. Finally, people shared their newly designed looks on social media!

RESULT: A new denim trend was born

Thousands of customised designs were created and a brand new denim trend was born.
Best of all, the sell-through rate of AEO’s destroyed denim line was over 85%.
