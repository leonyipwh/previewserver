Millennials are born into a world proliferated with connected devices. One in which instant gratification is always available and endlessly overwhelming. Sadly, this means that kids today are missing out on the process of creating. As the annual sponsor of Hong Kong’s biggest Mardi Gras for kids, Standard Chartered wondered: what if we could help kids discover the joy of creating in the real world?

Introducing The Robot Baker – an interactive experience that allows kids to design,
produce and play with their very own robots.

From choosing an original design through to bringing their creations come to life and on to their final assembly, everything was intentionally made transparent, so that kids could see
the beauty of the robot making from beginning to end.



Within 48 hours, more than 100,000 people visited the Robot Baker,
and over 30,000 robots were brought to life.
